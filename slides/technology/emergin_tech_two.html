COSO Enterprise Risk Management

COSO Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) is a framework that helps organizations identify, assess, and manage risks to achieve strategic objectives and improve decision-making. It integrates risk management into business processes through key components such as governance, strategy, performance, and information to enhance resilience and value creation.

IT Governance Framework Activity

Step One

Select one of the of the IT Governance frameworks (e.g., COBIT, ITIL, ISO/IEC 27001)

Step Two

Research online (company websites, case studies, and reports) to find examples of how a real company has implemented the framework

Step Three

Answer the following questions:
1. What are the key principles of this framework?
2. What types of organizations commonly use it?
3. What are the benefits and challenges of implementation?
4. Find at least one company using it and explain how they apply it.
