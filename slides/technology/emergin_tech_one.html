How would you define "Emerging Technology?

Variety of Definitions

Gartner
"an innovation that breaks into the market with the potential for significant business and social impact over the next five to ten years."

World Economic Forum
"discusses emerging technologies within the context of the "Fourth Industrial Revolution, technologies are seen as transformative, altering existing industries and creating new ones."

McKinsey and Company
"innovations that drive rapid change across industries, influencing business strategies and operations"

Forrestor
"those that are in the early stages of maturity but have the potential to become significant drivers of business innovation and disruption"

Lux Research
"technologies that have the potential to transform industries through scientific innovation"

Deloitte
"innovations that create or reshape industries and markets by introducing new capabilities or redefining existing ones."

Characteristics of Emerging Tech

Novelty
New technologies that can significantly change industries

Rapid Growth
Technologies that evolve quickly due to continuous innovation

Potential for Disruption
Technologies that can significantly change industries

Uncertainty
Long-term implications are not fully understood.

Scalability
The technology has the potential for widespread adoption

Working Definition

Emerging Technology refers to new, innovative, and rapidly evolving technologies that have the potential to significantly impact industries, economies, and society. They may introduce new capabilities, disrupt exiting markets, or create entirely new industries..

User Experience Research

Why User research?

A few User Research methods

Observations
Give people tasks or instructions and watch them use your design, without help.

Interviews
Ask prospective users questions about their expectations of your design.

Task Analysis
learning about user goals, and understand the tasks users will perform on your site.

Focus Groups
a moderated discussion where you can learn about users’ attitudes, beliefs, desires, and reactions to concepts.

Surveys
a structured questionnaire that your target audience completes over the internet generally through a filling out a form

What's involved in Market Analysis?

Market analysis research typically involves gathering and evaluating data to understand market conditions, trends, and consumer behavior.

Industry Analysis
Market Size and Location, Trends
Tools: Gartner, Forrester, Statista, Journals

Competitor Analysis
Direct + Indirect Competitors
Tools: SEMrush, SimilarWeb, Crunchbase

Strategic Analysis
Product/Service features and benefits, pricing strategy
Tools: Competitor Website, Comparisons Blogs, Lifecycle Analysis
